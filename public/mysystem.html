Welcome to the MySystem pre-prototype demo

What this example does:

  • Provides a field for composing a MySystem diagram
  • Provides example images of objects involved in energy transfers and transformations
  • Enables objects to be connected by colored and labeled arrows to show how energy is transferred
  • Allows arrow widths to be adjusted to depict the amount of energy transferred between objects

How to use it :

  • Drag and drop objects from the left-hand sidebar to the main field.
  • Connect the modules with arrows by dragging from one button to another.

Planned features for coming versions:

  • Diagrams will be able to be saved, shared and embedded into blogs or Web pages
  • Embedded assessment will be integrated into the diagramming process
  • A library of images will be available, with the ability to upload custom images
    • Objects will be able to contain subsystems, and students will be able to zoom in to view and edit these subsystems
  • Quantities of energy and efficiencies of transformation will be displayed, and energy transformations will be tracked and converted
  • More complex diagrams and time sequences will be able to be displayed, and the creation and evolution of diagrams will be able to be tracked and shown
